IMAGE REPLACEMENT GUIDE
=======================

Below is every placeholder image in the site. Replace each with your own photo at the specified dimensions for best results. All current placeholders are served from placehold.co — when you substitute your own images, save them into this folder and update the src path in the corresponding HTML file.

Recommended format: high-quality JPG for photos, SVG for logos/icons, WebP where supported for best performance.


HOME PAGE (index.html)
----------------------
- Hero background image (1920x1080) — a wide, editorial shot of a finished kitchen, bath, or living area. Set in the inline style on `.hero-bg` in assets/css/styles.css, or swap the URL directly in the style attribute.
- "Why Choose Us" section image (800x600) — team at work, a crew member in action, or a jobsite process shot.
- "Service Area Map" image (800x600) — a styled map of your coverage area.
- Blog preview cards (1200x630 each, 3 images) — feature photos pulled from your blog posts.
- Open Graph social preview image (1200x630) — what shows when the page is shared on Facebook/LinkedIn.


ABOUT PAGE (about.html)
-----------------------
- "Our Story" section image (800x600) — shop, showroom, or team portrait.
- Team member photos (400x400 each, 4 images) — square headshots in natural light.
- Certifications/affiliations logos (200x100 each, 6 images) — grayscale or full-color supplier, accreditation, or trade association logos.


SERVICES PAGE (services.html)
-----------------------------
- Service One through Six feature images (800x600 each, 6 images) — a "hero" photo for each service section.


SERVICE AREAS PAGE (service-areas.html)
---------------------------------------
- Service area map (1600x900) — a styled full-width map with your coverage highlighted.


GALLERY PAGE (gallery.html)
---------------------------
- 12 portfolio images, ideally a mix of 600x600, 600x700, 600x800, and 600x900 for visual variety in the masonry layout. Use your best completed projects — minimum 1200px wide recommended so lightbox images look sharp.


TESTIMONIALS PAGE (testimonials.html)
-------------------------------------
- No images required — uses initial-based avatars.


BLOG INDEX (blog.html)
----------------------
- Featured post image (1200x800 or 1200x630) — full-width feature image for the top blog post.
- Blog card thumbnails (1200x630 each) — match the featured images from each linked post.


BLOG POSTS (blog/post-1.html, post-2.html, post-3.html)
-------------------------------------------------------
- Featured image (1200x630) at the top of each post.
- Inline mid-article image (1200x630) roughly midway through each post.
- Author bio avatar (100x100) — a square portrait of the author.


CONTACT PAGE (contact.html)
---------------------------
- Map embed placeholder (1920x450) — replace the placeholder <img> with a real Google Maps <iframe> embed. A commented example iframe is left in the HTML for reference.


QUOTE PAGE (quote.html)
-----------------------
- No additional images required beyond shared elements.


FAVICON
-------
- assets/favicon.svg — a simple SVG house mark. Replace with your own logo icon (SVG preferred; 64x64 or square).


404 PAGE (404.html)
-------------------
- No images required — uses typographic design.


NOTES
-----
- All image `alt` attributes are currently written as instructional placeholder text (e.g. "Finished project 01 — replace with your project photo"). Update them to match the actual content of each image for accessibility and SEO.
- For Open Graph social preview images, keep the aspect ratio at 1.91:1 (1200x630 is the standard).
- Compress all photos before uploading. Target under 300 KB per image where possible. Tools: TinyPNG, Squoosh, ImageOptim.
