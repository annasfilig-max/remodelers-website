Frequently Asked Questions

General

We've been serving homeowners in our region for well over a decade. Across that time we've refined our process, grown a reliable crew, and completed hundreds of projects — from small updates to full whole-home renovations.

Yes. We carry full general liability insurance and workers' compensation, and hold the contractor licensing required in our jurisdiction. We're happy to provide current certificates of insurance upon request before any project begins.

We offer a complimentary first consultation where we visit your home, walk through the space with you, and talk through goals. Detailed design proposals may include a design fee depending on scope — we'll always be clear about costs before anything is signed.

We cover the greater service region surrounding our shop. See our Service Areas page for the full list of communities. If your town isn't listed, give us a call — we occasionally take projects just outside our standard region.

Service & Scheduling

We respond to new inquiries within one business day, usually sooner. Initial consultations can typically be scheduled within one to two weeks depending on our current project load and the season.

We primarily focus on planned renovations rather than emergency repairs, but if you're an existing client we'll always do our best to assist. For true emergencies we can often refer you to a trusted partner while coordinating any longer-term remodel work.

Our team will arrive on time, lay down floor protection, and keep a tidy workspace throughout the visit. You'll have a single point of contact who gives you a clear end-of-day update so you're never wondering what happened or what's next.

Yes. Our workmanship is backed by a written warranty, and all product materials carry the manufacturer's warranty on top of that. If something isn't right, we'll make it right.

Pricing & Estimates

We accept check, ACH bank transfer, and major credit cards. Deposit and progress payments follow a schedule spelled out in your signed agreement — you always know what's due and when.

We work with trusted third-party financing partners who offer home improvement loans with a range of terms. We'd be glad to share options with you during your consultation so you can compare what works best for your situation.

We use detailed, line-item fixed pricing based on the scope developed during the design phase. Once signed, your price doesn't change unless you request a change. This approach protects you from surprise add-ons and keeps the whole project predictable.

We can share ballpark ranges over the phone, but real pricing requires seeing the space in person. Every home and every project is different — accurate numbers come from accurate measurements, and we'd rather be right than fast.
